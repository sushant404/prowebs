Capital & Fund Design

Designing and managing capital vehicles that are built for complexity — not just financial return. This engagement covers the full arc of fund development: from thesis formation and structure design, through LP relationship development and capital formation, to portfolio construction and ongoing fund management.

Clients include fund managers, family offices, institutional allocators, foundations, and impact-first investors seeking to deploy capital with both rigor and contextual intelligence. Particular depth in climate technology, artificial intelligence, social innovation, and nature-based finance.

ADVISORY · STRATEGY · FUNDRAISING

Venture & Growth Advisory

Working with founders and operators across the full arc from ideation to exit. This is not generic mentorship — it is strategic partnership at the points where it matters most: sharpening the narrative before a fundraise, preparing for investor diligence, building the financial model that tells the right story, and navigating the organizational complexity that comes with growth.

Particular experience with early and growth-stage companies in climate technology, artificial intelligence, and social innovation. Also works with intrapreneurs and innovation leads inside larger organizations seeking to operate with startup agility.

SYSTEMS · AI · INTELLIGENCE

Intelligence & Systems

Organizations increasingly need to understand complex markets, build analytical tools, and integrate artificial intelligence into their decision-making — but most lack the combination of domain expertise and technical fluency to do it well.

This engagement bridges that gap. Working at the intersection of business systems analysis and AI capability, Sushant helps organizations define what they need to know, build the tools to know it, and design the workflows that make intelligence actionable. Outputs range from AI-powered dashboards and market intelligence products to full business systems redesign.

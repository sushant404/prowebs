CO-EDITOR FORTHCOMING PACIFIC INTEGRAL

Embracing Wholeness in Diversity

Towards Thriving Human Futures

"How can we cultivate wholeness in an increasingly divided world?"
"What if diversity isn't a challenge to overcome, but a path to human flourishing?"

Embracing Wholeness in Diversity explores how we can move beyond division and cultivate a deeper sense of integration within human diversity. Co-edited by Sushant Shrestha and Abigail Lynam, this book brings together insights from scholars, practitioners, and thought leaders in adult development, cultural pluralism, and social transformation.

Diversity is often framed as a problem to be solved. This book offers a different perspective — one that sees diversity as a wellspring of wisdom and creative potential. Through personal narratives, theoretical frameworks, and practical applications, contributors explore how to move beyond superficial inclusion toward genuine integration and collective healing.

This book stands out for its integrative approach — bridging traditional wisdom with contemporary social justice, individual psychology with collective transformation. It speaks to leaders, educators, coaches, and change agents working across cultural divides — anyone seeking to understand how we can cultivate more inclusive and life-affirming futures.
